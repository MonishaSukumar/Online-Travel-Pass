Terms and Conditions for Online Travel Pass

Bus pass:-

1. Student Studying in Evening College shall enclose Unemployment Certificate issued by concerned Head of the Institution.

2. Free bus passes are eligible for below 12 years for boys up to 7th class and girls below 18 years up to 10th class free bus passes will be issued only up to 30th September .

3. Concessional bus passes for the students will be allowed up to the age of 35yrs .

4. The entire image consisting of the photo along with the signature is required to be scanned and stored in *.jpg format on local machine.

5.Ensure that the size of the scanned image is not more than 50kb. If the size of the file is more than 50kb, then adjust the settings of the scanner such as the dpi resolution, no. of colours etc., during the process of scanning.


Train pass:-

QUARTERLY AND MONTHLY TICKETS FOR TRAVEL IN 1ST AND 2ND CLASS BETWEEN ANY TWO STATIONS ARE ISSUED SUBJECT TO THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS :

1.Season tickets must bear the signature or thumb impression of the holder, in the absence of which the ticket would be treated as invalid.

2.Season ticket holders are issued an identity card by the station issuing the season ticket and the season

3.Ticket is valid only with the identity card.

4.No alteration is permitted

5.Season ticket holders are not permitted to travel in reserved coaches and trains governed by distance restrictions.

6.Season tickets are not transferable.

7.Season tickets can be issued for a maximum distance of 150 kms.

8.Season tickets can be renewed 3 days in advance of the date of expiry of the earlier ticket.

9.Luggage up to a maximum of 15 Kgs. In 1st class and 10 Kgs. In 2nd class is permitted free of charge on one ticket. Excess luggage to be booked before commencement of journey otherwise penal charges will be levied.


SEASON TICKET FOR STUDENTS :

1.Season tickets to students are issued for First and Second Class up to a maximum distance of 150 Kms. The student monthly season tickets will be charged at half of the normal adult season ticket fare and quarterly season tickets: on payment of 2.7 times of the concessional fares of Monthly Season Tickets vide (I) above. These MSTs/QSTs will be issued without any minimum charge to the student, to whom the tickets are issued, should not exceed 25 years in the case of student in general, 27 years in the case of students belonging to the Scheduled Caste/Tribes and 35 years in the case of Research Scholars.

NOTE : Season tickets to students of scheduled castes and scheduled tribes shall be issued as under : Monthly Season Tickets: On payment of 50% below the normal student Monthly Season Ticket fare. Quarterly Season Tickets: On payment of 2.7 times of the concessional fares of Monthly Season Tickets vide (I) above. Free second class ordinary monthly season tickets to students studying in classes up to 10th standard for commuting daily between the stations serving their place of residence and school, shall be issued subject to all other conditions of monthly season tickets to students.
